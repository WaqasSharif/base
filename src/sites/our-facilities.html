NORTH SUFFOLK CHAPEL

Baker-Foster Funeral home opened in 1992 and is dedicated to serving those who live in North Suffolk and the surrounding areas.The 11,000 square foot building features a 200-seat chapel, 50-seat family room, viewing rooms, selection room, offices, and a preparation area. The foyer offers a home-like atmosphere and tranquil warmth of a fireplace, antique mantel, and pump organ. The chapel has a historic stained glass window from Main Street United Methodist Church.
