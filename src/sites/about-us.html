Harris-Nadeau Mortuary is proud to be locally owned and operated. We treat every family like they are our family. One person serves you from start to finish, helping you with every detail. No funeral home in Spartanburg or Cherokee county provides the quality of care, ease of arrangements and breadth of funeral or cremation services at the prices we do. Instead of enriching shareholders, we invest in our community.
