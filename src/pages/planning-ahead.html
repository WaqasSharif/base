Handling Finances for Your Funeral: Benefits of Pre-Planning

Are you anxious about the rising costs of funerals? You’re not alone. Funeral pre-planning is all about giving you peace of mind. You may set up monthly payments now or arrange for one lump sum from your bank account or trust fund to pay for your funeral costs when the time comes. One of the biggest benefits of pre-planning is paying ahead and locking in the current funeral costs without worrying about future inflation. The money allocated for your funeral is also non-taxable.
