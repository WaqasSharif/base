Our Funeral Services

When it comes time to arrange a funeral for a loved one who has passed, it can be hard to know where to begin. Our staff can assist you through the process, addressing any questions or concerns you may have, and offering a comprehensive range of funeral services. We’re here to respect your wishes and remain sensitive to your needs, so you pay your respects in the way you and your loved one intended. Our helpful funeral planning services include:

  • Pre-Arrangements: Many people decide to take their funeral planning into their own hands with our pre-arrangement services. Planning your own service helps take some of the weight off your loved one's shoulders and will ensure your funeral meets your exact final wishes.
  • Traditional Funeral and Burial: If you’re looking for a traditional funeral ceremony, we can provide a step-by-step schedule and can organize a casket burial in a plot of your choice.
  • Personalized Funeral Options: If you’d like to arrange a funeral ceremony that’s as unique as your loved one, we can help. Honoring those who have passed is our top priority, and our customizable solutions can help accommodate any personal, cultural, or religious requests.
  • Cremation: As a cost-effective and flexible memorial type, our cremation services give you the choice of how your loved one’s ashes will be treated. Whether their wish was to have their remains scattered in a memorial garden or placed in an urn, we can help.
  • Memorial Ceremony: A memorial ceremony allows the deceased person's loved ones to pay their respects whenever it’s most convenient. Whether it’s shortly after passing or weeks later at a designated time, we can make arrangements that adhere to your unique needs.
  • Caskets, Urns, and Memorial Markers: When you choose us for funeral services, we offer a wide selection of urns, caskets, and memorial markers in a variety of styles and materials.
  • Flowers and Memorial Donations: Sending flowers is a thoughtful way to remember and honor a loved one, and our compassionate staff can help establish guidelines for memorial donations.
